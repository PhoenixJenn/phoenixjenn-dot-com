How to get coordinates Google provides a Geocoding Web Service which should do what you're looking for. You will be able to do geocoding on your server. JSON Example: http://maps.google.com/maps/api/geocode/json?address=1600+Amphitheatre+Parkway,+Mountain+View,+CA&sensor=false
https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/geocoding/start?csw=1

http://www.gps-coordinates.net/
https://support.google.com/maps/answer/18539?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en
Documentation: http://www.w3schools.com/googleapi/google_maps_ref.asp
https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/geocode/json?address=1600+Amphitheatre+Parkway,+Mountain+View,+CA&key=YOUR_API_KEY
