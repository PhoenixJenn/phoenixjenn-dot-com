Creator

Foreword

I like to think of myself as a renaissance girl. Technically, I'm a software engineer, but I dabble in the arts as well. I work hard to play hard... always dreaming up new projects and adventures...always pushing my physical, intellectual and creative limits. I make annual lists of goals, post them publicly, and do everything that I can to complete them.

Life is short, and I believe that if something doesn't work, do something else.

I created this site as a playground for projects that I've always wanted to work on, as well as a showcase for some of my adventures. This site is meant to be a work in progress. I've even left myself To Do notes on the pages! After 2 years of working on it for a few minutes here and there, I decided to launch it as is. It isn't perfect, but that was never the intention.

So here we are. On September, 17, 2017, we have lift-off. I welcome feedback and new ideas.

Fitness

I've always been an off-beat athlete, interested in fitness activities where you are only competing against yourself. Running, cycling, skating, martial arts, etc. Although I believe that fitness is a personal journey, the science of excercise and nutrition intrigued me enough to dabble in personal training, group exercise. Oh, and I may or may not have also gotten a Masters in Exercise Science, focusing on Sport Psychology.

Creative

I have a wild creative streak. I like to paint, bake, draw, make jewelry. Anything that allows me to create, makes me happy.
