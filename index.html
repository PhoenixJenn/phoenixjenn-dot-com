Foreword

I like to think of myself as a renaissance girl. Technically, I'm a software engineer, but I dabble in the arts as well. I work hard to play hard... always dreaming up new projects and adventures...always pushing my physical, intellectual and creative limits. I make annual lists of goals, post them publicly, and do everything that I can to complete them. Life is short, and I believe that if something doesn't work, do something else. I created this site as a playground for projects that I've always wanted to work on, as well as a showcase for some of my adventures. This site is meant to be a work in progress. I've even left myself To Do notes on the pages! After 2 years of working on it for a few minutes here and there, I decided to launch it as is. It isn't perfect, but that was never the intention. So here we are. On September, 17, 2017, we have lift-off. I welcome feedback and new ideas.
